Healthcare for Every Village

A low-cost, multilingual and low-bandwidth telemedicine platform designed for rural communities.

Why This Matters

In and around Nabha, thousands of rural residents from over 173 villages struggle to access timely healthcare. Long travel distances, limited medical staff, unavailable specialists, and medicine shortages force daily-wage earners and farmers to delay or completely avoid treatment.

These delays often lead to preventable health complications, financial losses, and a decline in overall community well-being. Healthcare should not depend on location, connectivity, or income.

Helixa is designed to bridge this gap by delivering low-cost, multilingual, and low-bandwidth telemedicine directly to rural communities. With offline-first access, secure digital records, and smart consultations, Helixa brings reliable healthcare closer to home.

Core Features

Smart Video Consultation

Low-bandwidth audio and video consultations that dynamically adapt to rural network conditions.

Digital Medical Records

Secure patient records stored locally and synced automatically when connectivity returns.

Offline-First Access

Enables healthcare access even without internet connectivity using local device storage.

Medicine Availability

Real-time updates on essential medicines available at nearby local pharmacies.

AI Symptom Checker

Lightweight hybrid AI combining medical rules and predictive assistance for early guidance.

Multilingual & Voice Support

Regional language support and voice prompts designed for elderly and first-time users.

Project Scope

Helixa is designed as a scalable, offline-first telemedicine platform that adapts to real rural healthcare challenges.
