### Offline-First Telemedicine PWA for Rural Healthcare

Helixa is a modern, low-bandwidth, multilingual **Progressive Web Application (PWA)** designed to improve access to essential healthcare services in rural and underserved regions, starting with Nabha and surrounding villages.

The platform focuses on **offline-first healthcare delivery**, enabling patients to consult doctors, manage medical records, and access medicine availability even in areas with unreliable or no internet connectivity.

## ğŸš© Problem Statement

Rural communities across India face serious healthcare challenges:
- Long travel distances to hospitals
- Shortage of doctors and specialists
- Unavailable medicines
- Poor network connectivity
- Financial loss for daily-wage earners due to delayed treatment

These issues often lead to **preventable complications** and reduced quality of life.

## ğŸ’¡ Solution Overview

**Helixa** bridges this gap by bringing healthcare closer to villages through a lightweight, accessible, and scalable telemedicine platform.

The system is designed to:
- Work reliably in low-bandwidth environments
- Store data offline and sync automatically when connectivity returns
- Support regional languages and voice prompts for elderly users
- Scale across different state healthcare infrastructures
